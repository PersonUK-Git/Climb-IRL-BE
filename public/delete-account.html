Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you delete your ClimbIRL account, all your data will be permanently removed.

Warning: This action is permanent. You will lose your rank, XP, level status, and all completed task history. This cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly through the mobile application:

  • Open the ClimbIRL app.
  • Go to your Profile tab.
  • Scroll down to the Settings & Legal section.
  • Tap on Delete Account.
  • Confirm the deletion in the dialog.

Manual Deletion Request

If you no longer have access to the app and wish to request manual deletion of your data, please email us at support@climbirl.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.
