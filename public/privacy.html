Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At ClimbIRL, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, username, email address, and profile data. We also track your progress, XP, and completed tasks to provide the core leaderboard and gamification features.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the ClimbIRL service.
  • Process your achievements and update your level.
  • Maintain the global leaderboard rankings.
  • Communicate with you about updates and features.

3. Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, all your personal data, XP history, and task records will be permanently removed from our active databases.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@climbirl.com.
